About Affiliate Innovators Limited

Pioneering technology solutions in Northern Ghana since 2018

Our History

From humble beginnings to a leading tech company

Building Tech Solutions for Tamale and Beyond

Affiliate Innovators Limited was founded in 2018 with a vision to bridge the digital divide in Northern Ghana. Starting with just three passionate tech enthusiasts working from a small office, we recognized the growing need for digital solutions among local businesses.

Our first major project was developing a website for a local agricultural cooperative, which helped them reach new markets and increase their revenue by 30%. This success fueled our growth and allowed us to expand our team and services.

In 2020, we moved to our current location at Jiso Plaza, where we established a modern workspace and training facility. This move enabled us to launch our tech training programs, empowering youth in Tamale with valuable digital skills.

Today, AIL serves over 200 clients across Northern Ghana and has successfully completed more than 300 projects. Our team has grown to 15 dedicated professionals specializing in various aspects of technology and digital solutions.
